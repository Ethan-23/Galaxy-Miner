Galaxy Miner

The game for intergalactic gamers

Login/Register? Save your progress

With our login and register feature, you don't have to worry about what happens when you close your browser because we will keep track of your progress while you're away!! All you have to do, if you haven't already is, register with us and we'll keep all of you're progress waiting right here for your return!!

Resources, Resources, Resources

To get resource material from the planet, all you have to do is click! One click gets you one piece of resource from the planet, so the more you click the more you get. Get clicking!!!

Upgrades!!!

Want to level up your game?? Well, with your resources that you collect, you can purchase upgrades! These upgrades can give you an edge on collecting as many resources as you can. You can get more resources per click, have your clicker do all the work by adding a click per second, and so much more!!
